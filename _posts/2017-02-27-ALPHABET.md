## ALPHABET

Inspired by [36 Days of Type](http://www.36daysoftype.com/), I decided to create a series where I'll animate each letter of the alphabet. The aim of this series is to experiment with various styles and design principals, and learn more about the principles of typography.

To see the series progression, click the grid icon in the top right of the screen.
