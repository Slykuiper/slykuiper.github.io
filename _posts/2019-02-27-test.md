### The Beginning

In August 2017, my first project for Streamlabs involved animating 16 existing icons for their website. We wanted to experiment with [Bodymovin](https://slykuiper.com/bodymovin/) for this project, so I delivered the 16 animated icons, each with a daymode and nightmode variant, as .json as well as .webm as a failsafe.

### Library Genesis

 With the developement of Streamlabs OBS underway, Streamlabs was moving rapidly in an exciting direction. One core strategy for user acquisition was the Library - a page on the site where streamers can browse and install themes. A lot of streamers don't have the design skills to make their own stream graphics, or the money to buy or commission a unique package. The Library offers over a thousand themes freely available to any Streamlabs OBS user
