## LIVESTREAMS!

I used to stream on Twitch a while ago and plan on picking it back up. I figure it would be a great way to stay on task and avoid distractions when working on projects, as well as giving viewers the opportunity to ask questions and learn new things by following my process for creating graphics. It's a good open platform for discussion, bouncing ideas off eachother and sharing inspirations that we find. You can follow my [Twitch Channel](https://www.twitch.tv/slykuiper) and opt-in for email notifications to be notified when I go live.
