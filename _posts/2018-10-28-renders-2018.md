This is an assorted collection of renders I've made in 2018 documenting my journey through Cinema4D. These don't belong to any project in particular, but are rather a way for me to learn the various skills involved with C4D. Playing with different modeling techniques, lighting setups, and render engines on one-off renders allows me to learn something new with every artwork. It's a fun challenge that builds my technical skills to apply them on professional projects.

You can click on any grid item to be taken to the high resolution, if available.
