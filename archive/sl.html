STREAMLABS

STREAMER IN ALL OF US

Intent

Instead of directly promoting streamlabs products/features, this would be an animated storytelling sequence that appeals to streamers on a more personal level. The intent is to relate to streamers' personal experiences and feelings, as well as subtely tie in our products (slobs, alerts, facemasks, whatever else). The mood is more genuine and wholesome rather than flashy/salesy, meaning the products will show up naturally to appear as routine features that streamers commonly use and not presented as something new to try out. For example, in the last slide, alerts are popping out of the screen and the streamer glances at the screen and acknowledges the event. Another example slide could be a facemask popping up over the streamers face - on top of the figure as if it's really in the environment, not code appearing on top of a webcam filter inside slobs.

Visual Style

Mixed media collaboration incorporating simple 3D assets with simple materials and lighting, 2D motion graphics and frame-by-frame animation.
