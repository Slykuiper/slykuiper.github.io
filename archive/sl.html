STREAMER IN ALL OF US

STREAMLABS CAMPAIGN

Intent

Instead of directly promoting streamlabs products and features, this would be a short animated storytelling sequence that appeals to streamers on a more personal level. It goes through the motions of a streamer before and during their stream. The intent is to relate to streamers' personal experiences and feelings, as well as subtely tie in our products (slobs, alerts, facemasks, whatever else). The mood is more genuine and wholesome rather than flashy/salesy, meaning the products will show up naturally to appear as routine features that streamers commonly use and not presented as something new to try out.

For example, in the last slide, alerts are popping out of the screen and the streamer glances at the screen and acknowledges the event. Another example slide could be a facemask popping up over the streamers face - on top of the figure as if it's really in the environment, not code appearing on top of a webcam filter inside slobs. Another scene idea is a rapidly changing sequence with different characters streaming different categories (fps games, moba, just chatting, irl/vlog) for diversity and to show anyone can be a streamer.

The visual style is a mixed media collaboration incorporating simple 3D assets with simple materials and lighting, 2D motion graphics and frame-by-frame animation. The first three frames are further developed than the rest to closer depict the visual style. Hover over each frame to see the scene's direction.
